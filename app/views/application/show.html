David Heinemeier Hansson Ruby on Rails Creator

David Heinemeier Hansson was born in 1979 in Copenhagen, Denmark. After graduation from
Copenhagen Business School in 2005, he moved to Chicago, Illinois, USA.
Hansson is a partner in 37signals, the company behind Basecamp, Highrise, Backpack,
Writeboard, and Ta-da List. 37signals runs a popular weblog at Signal vs Noise. In 2005, Hansson
was recognized by Google and O'Reilly with the Hacker of the Year award for his creation and
development of Ruby on Rails.

Location: Chicago
